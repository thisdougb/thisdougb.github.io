As a freelance DevOps engineer I work on all sizes of projects.
I get to work with start-ups who've survived long enough to have serious funding, and corporates in the midst of so-called _digital transformation_.
What I want to talk about in this blog post is the common fork in the road all projects seem to have trouble with.

Choosing infrastructure tooling, specifically for orchestration, seems to have become quite problematic.
Tribes form, positions become entrenched, and perspective often evaporates.
