Basic ingredients to buy weekly.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-1.jpg)

Split ingredients into two, and chop.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-2.jpg)

Half in a bowl for now, half in a Tupperware and into the fridge.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-3.jpg)

Sprinkle a teaspoon of Oregano, and a tablespoon of Greek Kalamata olive oil.  Do not skimp on the olive oil quality.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-4.jpg)

The Tupperware should be in the fridge, only the bowl to eat now gets the oregano/oil dressing.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-5.jpg)

Full fat feta, don't skimp with the false economy of reduced rubbish. Kalamata olives only, to go with the oil.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-6.jpg)

Cut the feta in half, then cut one half in half again. One block of feta does four Greek salads.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-7.jpg)

Done.
![weekly ingredients](/images/greek-salad-8.jpg)
